Contract Review

Project information

  • Programming Language Use: PHP, Javascript, JQuery, CSS, Boostrap
  • Framework: CodeIgniter (MVC)
  • Database: MYSQL

Project Desciption ( Scope )

  • CRUD for Contract Review
  • Assign Supervisor and Management to conduct the review
  • Send email to supervisor
  • Supervisor will receive email with link and encrypted password
  • Supervisor add a review. Score 1-10 and reason for that review
  • Management will receive email after the supervisor reviewed the contractor
  • Management can override the scores
  • Generate PDF. Send final report to contractor, HR, Supervisor and Management
